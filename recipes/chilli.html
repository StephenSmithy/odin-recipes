One Pot Chilli Con Carne

Description

This beautiful one pot chilli recipe is simple, versatile, and great for feeding a family of four on a tight budget. Once you have made it a few times you will be free to explore, adding in new vegetables and experimenting with different seasonings.

You will need a dutch oven for this one pot recipe; however, if you don't have one to hand you can start this meal in a frying pan and transfer to a lidded pot in the oven.

Ingredients

  • 500g beef mince
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 400g red kidney beans, drained
  • 400g chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp ground cumin

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 150c and warm the dutch oven over a high heat.
  2. Add a teaspoon of oil to the pan and brown the mince on a high heat.
  3. Once browned, throw in the diced onion, reduce the heat and cook until the onions start to become translucent.
  4. Add the chopped bell pepper and cook for another couple of minutes.
  5. Mix the paprika, chilli powder, oregano and ground cumin in a bowl. Scatter over the cooked mince and stir.
  6. Immediately add the chopped tomatoes and stir again, bring to a slow boil and then reduce the heat.
  7. Stir in the kidney beans.
  8. Put the lid on the dish or cover tightly with tin foil and transfer to the oven for 45 minutes.
  9. Serve with rice and dress the dish with coriander if desired.
